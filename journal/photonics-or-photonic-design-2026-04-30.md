>
> First of all, what is a beamformer? (simple explanation) : 
>
> Imagine you want to send a super-fast internet signal (5G, future 6G, or satellite connection) to a phone, a house, or a satellite.
>
> A regular radio signal spreads out in all directions, like a light bulb lighting up an entire room. It works, but it’s very inefficient. You waste a lot of energy, the signal weakens quickly over distance, and there are lots of interferences with other devices around.
>
> A beamformer (literally “beam former”) is like an ultra-precise smart projector:
> Instead of spreading everywhere, it concentrates all the signal power into a very narrow directional beam pointed exactly at your phone or your house.
>
