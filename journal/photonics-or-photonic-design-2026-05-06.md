> A beamformer (literally “beam former”) is like an ultra-precise smart projector:
> Instead of spreading everywhere, it concentrates all the signal power into a very narrow directional beam pointed exactly at your phone or your house.
>
> Result? The connection is much faster, much more stable, reaches much farther… and uses way less energy.
