In AI clusters, network reliability is critical, and since GPUs remain the dominant cost driver, some architectures may view light-source cost savings as less relevant than proven performance and qualification history. #siliconphotonics provides an architectural shift by offering discrete optical components toward integration, packaging, thermal management, DSP/FEC, test, and system-level optimization.
