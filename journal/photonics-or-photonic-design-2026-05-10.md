>
> $COHR - Coherent

>
> $SIVE - Sivers Semiconductors
> RF and laser components for optical transceivers. Small, Swedish.
>
> $MRVL - Marvell Technology
> Custom silicon and DSPs for high-speed optical transmission. Nvidia took an equity position. The market is starting to price this differently.
>
> $SMOP - Smartoptics
> Optical networking for edge and mid-market deployments. Q1 was misread. The $0.5M one-time cost was a 4,100 sqm expansion in Kista, just outside Stockholm, on a 7-year lease. Structural demand, not operational weakness.

> Six positions across the stack.
