>
> The reason the re-rating hasn't happened yet is also the reason the opportunity exists.
>

That three-step removal makes the AI narrative difficult to pitch in a two-page equity note. Investors cannot quantify what they cannot measure, so they do not pay for it.

The February selloff is a live example of how fast the market punishes MLI when copper softens. There is no named hyperscaler contract announcement to wait for.

You cannot build the AI infrastructure stack without it. And the only vertically integrated domestic manufacturer of copper products trades at a 36% discount to its industrial peers with $1.39 billion in cash, zero debt, record earnings, and a rising dividend.
